# SEIS739_FinalProject
A comprehensive web application for managing commercial/industrial drone operations including fleet tracking, mission planning, maintenance scheduling, pilot management, and regulatory compliance.

#Product Vision Statement
For commercial drone operators, industrial inspection companies, and aerial service providers
Who need to efficiently manage multiple drones, ensure regulatory compliance, maintain airworthiness, and maximize fleet utilization while minimizing downtime and operational risks
The SkyControl Pro is a comprehensive drone fleet management platform
That provides real-time fleet visibility, automated maintenance scheduling, regulatory compliance tracking, and mission optimization to increase operational efficiency by 40% and reduce unplanned downtime by 60%
Unlike generic asset management systems or spreadsheet-based tracking that lack aviation-specific workflows and fail to address FAA compliance requirements
Our product is purpose-built for commercial drone operations with integrated airspace authorization tracking, automated pilot certification validation, battery health monitoring, and pre-flight inspection workflows that ensure every mission is safe, legal, and optimally resourced
